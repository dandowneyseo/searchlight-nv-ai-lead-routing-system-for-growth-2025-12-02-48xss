Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Searchlight's AI lead routing system

How does the AI lead routing system work?

Our AI lead routing system uses machine learning algorithms to analyze multiple data points including lead characteristics, team member expertise, historical conversion rates, availability, and performance metrics. When a new lead comes in, the system evaluates all available team members and routes the lead to the person most likely to successfully convert it. The system continuously learns from outcomes, improving its routing decisions over time to maximize your conversion rates and revenue.

Can Searchlight integrate with our existing CRM and tools?

Yes! Searchlight is built with integration in mind. We offer pre-built integrations with popular CRM platforms like Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and many others. Our API-first architecture also allows for custom integrations with virtually any business tool you're currently using. Our technical team can work with you to ensure a seamless integration process. Most implementations are up and running within 24-48 hours.

What kind of ROI can we expect from using Searchlight?

Our customers typically see a 30-50% increase in conversion rates within the first 90 days. Many report reducing their customer acquisition cost by 40-60% while simultaneously increasing lead quality. The exact ROI depends on your industry, current processes, and team size. We've seen businesses save $50,000-$500,000+ annually by eliminating ad waste and improving conversion efficiency. Most of our clients see positive ROI within the first month of implementation.

How is my lead data secured and protected?

Security is our top priority. Searchlight uses enterprise-grade encryption for all data in transit and at rest. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other major data protection regulations. Our infrastructure is hosted on secure, redundant servers with automatic backups. We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing. Your data is never shared with third parties without explicit consent. We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification and provide detailed security documentation upon request.

We provide comprehensive onboarding and training for all customers. This includes system setup, team training, and optimization consultation. You'll have access to our dedicated support team via email, chat, and phone. We also provide detailed documentation, video tutorials, and best practice guides.
